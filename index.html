Who Can Contribute:

  • Alumni

    Since its founding, President University has produced more than 11.000 alumni spread across the world. Alumni are a very valuable asset for President University. The active participation of alumni in the progress of their alma mater is highly expected.

  • Industry

    Industry is a strategic partner for President University. Industry will later absorb the human resources produced by President University. Therefore, President University really hopes that Industry will participate in investing in human resource development in the future through President University donations.

  • Organization

    Organizations or communities can also contribute to the progress of President University. Various organizations and communities have shown their commitment to raising funds for President University.

  • Academic Community

    Both lecturers and staff are also expected to be able to contribute, either donating funds or initiating fundraising movements in order to increase the President University Endowment Fund.

  • Public

    President University really hopes for joint contributions from the wider community who care about higher education. Any individual can contribute by donating some of their funds for the development of science and technology.

The aims of Endowment Fund

This endowment fund is designed to provide support for the continuity of President University's operations, not only for the current generation but also for the future. With the determination to maintain the continuity of existence across generations, the management of these funds will be carried out carefully, ensuring that the benefits can be enjoyed by future generations. Some of the objectives of this endowment fund are as attached below:

Providing Scholarships

Part of the funds from the Endowment Fund will be used to provide scholarships to students with achievements and potential, ensuring access to quality education without financial barriers.

Academic and Research Development

Funds will be allocated to support research activities, seminars and workshops to improve academic excellence and produce innovations that are beneficial to society.

Facilities and Infrastructure Development

A portion of the funds will be invested in developing campus facilities and infrastructure, creating a modern and adequate learning environment.
